# CRIMEGPT 2.0

## AI-Powered Investigation, Documentation & Legal Intelligence Platform

---

# PROJECT OVERVIEW

CrimeGPT 2.0 is an AI-powered Investigation Operating System designed for Indian law enforcement agencies. The platform reduces paperwork, automates investigation documentation, assists officers with legal intelligence, manages evidence securely, maintains structured case diaries, and generates court-ready documents from FIR registration to chargesheet preparation.

The system is designed specifically to maximize hackathon evaluation criteria:

* Accuracy of Document Generation
* Legal Section Intelligence
* User Experience & Workflow
* Case Diary Management
* Multilingual Support
* Innovation
* Security & Compliance

---

# CORE PROBLEM BEING SOLVED

Current police investigations suffer from:

* Duplicate data entry across multiple documents
* Manual paperwork
* Inconsistent legal section mapping
* Poor evidence organization
* Lack of structured case diaries
* Time-consuming document generation
* Limited multilingual support
* Delayed investigation workflows

CrimeGPT solves this by creating a single source of truth for every case and automating repetitive legal and documentation tasks.

# FINAL PITCH

CrimeGPT 2.0 is an AI-powered Investigation Operating System that assists law enforcement officers from FIR registration to chargesheet generation by automating documentation, organizing evidence, recommending legal sections, maintaining structured case diaries, generating court-ready documents, supporting multilingual workflows, and ensuring security through encryption, audit trails, chain-of-custody tracking, and evidence integrity verification.

The platform reduces paperwork, improves legal accuracy, accelerates investigations, and enables smarter, technology-driven policing.
